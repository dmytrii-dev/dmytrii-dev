Privacy policy.

How personal data is handled when you visit this website or contact me about custom automation work.

If you contact me by email, I may process the information you choose to send, such as your name, email address, company or shop name, message content, workflow description, screenshots, sample data, exports, or other project details.

Why data is processed

Personal data may be used to respond to inquiries, discuss possible automation projects, provide agreed custom work, keep necessary business records, and maintain website security.

Legal basis

Depending on the situation, processing may be based on steps before entering into a contract, performance of a contract, legitimate interest in business communication and website security, or legal obligations such as tax and accounting record keeping.

Service providers

Personal data may be processed through providers used for website hosting, email, business communication, accounting, payment, or project delivery. Personal data is not sold.

Retention

Inquiry emails are kept only as long as reasonably needed for communication and business follow-up. Project, invoice, accounting, tax, and legal records may be kept for longer where required by law or necessary to protect legitimate business interests.

Cookies and analytics

At the time of the latest update, this website does not intentionally use analytics cookies, advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or a user account system. If analytics or marketing tools are added later, this policy should be updated.

Your rights

Under applicable data protection law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, object to processing, or request a copy of your personal data. You can contact me at hello@dmytrii.dev.

Supervisory authority

You may also contact the Slovak data protection supervisory authority if you believe your personal data has been processed unlawfully.
