Method

I work directly with the workflow.

The goal is not to force a generic automation idea onto your store. The goal is to understand the repeated process and build around how the work actually happens.

Understand

I look at where the data comes from, where it goes, and which steps repeat often enough to matter.

Build

The first version focuses on a clear workflow, real pages, test data, and a practical result.

Refine

After testing, I adjust behavior, edge cases, and reliability based on real usage.

For custom work, projects usually require a clear scope, test access or sample data, and staged payment. See the process page for how I normally work.
